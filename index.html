About me

I'm a product designer obsessed with making healthcare more human. My approach combines strategic thinking with meticulous craftsmanship — every pixel serves a purpose.

For the past 3 years, I've worked at the intersection of AI and healthcare, translating complex medical concepts into elegant, accessible interfaces. My background in traditional Indian art taught me that constraint breeds clarity.

Currently based in Lehi, Utah, open to remote opportunities and excited about building products that actually improve lives.

  • Design: Product design, UX research, design systems, accessibility
  • Tools: Figma, Framer, JavaScript, Webflow
  • Passionate about: Healthcare, AI ethics, design education
