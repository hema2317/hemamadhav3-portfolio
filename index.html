About me

I design for high-stakes human moments.

I am a Product Designer and founder with 3+ years shipping AI-powered products in health and education — two of the most consequential spaces where design decisions genuinely matter.

My background is unusual: a Master's in Biomedical Sciences from the University of Helsinki, clinical compliance at Beckton Dickinson, and then a full pivot into product design when I realised I wanted to build the tools, not just use them.

I work at the intersection of AI capability and human trust. The best AI design is invisible — it feels helpful, never scary, and always leaves the human in control.
